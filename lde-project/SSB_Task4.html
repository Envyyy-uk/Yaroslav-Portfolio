Task 4: Communication and Collaboration Analysis

Introduction

Modern businesses rely heavily on digital tools to support hybrid and remote work. Effective communication and online collaboration solutions are critical to ensuring business continuity [10].

1. Microsoft Teams (Communications application)

Microsoft Teams is a unified platform integrating chat, video, and files. Its key value lies in consolidating disparate communication streams [11].

  • Channel Communication: Replaces internal email, reducing information overload.
  • Synchronous Interaction: Features like recording and transcription overcome geographical barriers.
  • Integration: Deep integration with Microsoft 365 minimises "context switching."

2. Microsoft OneDrive (Online Collaboration Software)

Microsoft OneDrive is a cloud-based storage service critical for content collaboration [12].

  • Co-authoring: Allows real-time simultaneous editing, eliminating "versioning" problems.
  • Files On-Demand: Optimises local storage resources.
  • Security: Features complete version history and granular access controls [13].
